## Safety statements

**NOTICE — Intended use.**
Alert: This is an indoor computer keyboard powered from a USB 5 V port only.
Failure: Using other power, or outdoor/wet use, can damage it or cause malfunction.
Escape: Connect only to a standard USB-C host/port; use indoors, in dry conditions.

**WARNING — Small parts (ISO 7010 W001 + small-parts mark).**
Alert: Contains small parts (keycaps, stems, switches, screws).
Failure: Choking hazard if swallowed by young children.
Escape: Not a toy. Keep the product and its small parts away from children under 3 years.

**CAUTION — Sharp edges.**
Alert: The metal plate/case may have sharp edges or corners.
Failure: Cuts or abrasion when handling, especially during assembly.
Escape: Handle with care; wear protection when assembling a kit.

**CAUTION — Liquids (ISO 7010 P010 — do not expose to water).**
Alert: The keyboard is not water-resistant.
Failure: Spills can cause a short or malfunction.
Escape: Keep liquids away. If wet, disconnect immediately and let it dry fully before reuse.

**NOTICE — Electrostatic discharge (ESD).**
Alert: The displays and electronics can be sensitive to static discharge.
Failure: A static shock may cause a temporary glitch or, rarely, component damage.
Escape: Touch a grounded object before handling exposed electronics; avoid very dry/static environments.

**CAUTION — Kit assembly (kit version only).**
Alert: Assembly is mechanical only (inserting hot-swap switches/keycaps, connecting FPC cables) — **no
soldering is required**, as optional solder-in components are not shipped.
Failure: Minor pinch injuries, or a non-compliant build if instructions are not followed.
Escape: Follow the assembly guide, and do not substitute non-equivalent parts or modify the design —
modifications void CE conformity.

**NOTICE — Comfort.**
Alert: Prolonged typing can cause strain.
Failure: Discomfort or repetitive-strain symptoms.
Escape: Set up the split/tenting for a neutral posture and take regular breaks.

## Disposal (WEEE)  ♻ (crossed-out wheelie-bin)
Do not dispose of this product with household waste. Hand it in at an appropriate electrical-waste
collection point for recycling.
